Highlighted Features

Semantic Search

Finds decisions and legislation based on the meaning (vector search) of the legal problem, not just keywords.

RAG-Powered Answers

Bases answers on real legal texts in the database to prevent LLM hallucinations.

Microservice Architecture

Highly scalable and modern infrastructure with React, Java Spring Boot, and Python (FastAPI + LangChain) services.
